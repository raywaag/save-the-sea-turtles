Ways to Support

Donate a Boat

We accept boat donations to provide to our sea expeditions and research trips. Donating a boat can help you reduce docking costs, hassle to sell and deduct taxes weighing your unused boat down.

Adopt a Nest

The journey from laying to hatching to sea is an ongoing protection project. Adopting a nest provides the budget to protect the nest and its hatchlings from the egg births to their venture into the sea.

Donate Spare Change

Every penny goes towards saving our seas by researching, analyzing, protecting and oceanic rehabilitation. Any spare change helps make a wave.

Volunteer

We can always use help with caretaking, nest protection, clean-ups and sea restorations. Gain hours and lend a hand to the beaches that surround us.

Elite Sea Turtle Society

The Elite Sea Turtle Society is a community that continues giving to promote, advocate and sponsor research and expeditions. In fact, we would not be able to continue doing what we love without this community.
