Why Donate a Boat

THE NATIONAL SAVE THE SEA TURTLE FOUNDATION, INC. (NSTSTF) was established in 1987 for the protection of our fragile environment, promotion of research and scientific investigation and to contribute to environmental education, protection and public awareness in our communities.

NATIONAL SAVE THE SEA TURTLE FOUNDATION is a section 501 (c) (3) tax exempt charity as defined by the U.S. Internal Revenue Service.

Since its inception in 1987 the National Save The Sea Turtle Foundation has accepted donations of vessels to support our efforts to protect the marine environment and save sea turtles from extinction.

Yacht donations and charitable cash donations have paved the way to the Foundation’s 26 years of success.

Benefits of Boat Donation

Support

Become a part of a national effort to protect our marine environment and save the sea turtles.

Seller's Relief

End all expenses and the aggravation involved in the sale of your vessel by donating your boat.

Stop Paying

Dockage fees, insurance rates, fuel costs and maintenance - get rid of it.

Tax Benefits

Receive tax benefits without paying a sales commission.

Guidelines

It is the donor’s responsibility to obtain an appraisal from a “qualified appraiser”.

It is recommended that the donor engage a professional tax advisor before making any commitment regarding donation of property to a non-profit organization.

The National Save The Sea Turtle Foundation will provide documentation to allow the donation to qualify for the appraised value which will include Form 1098-C, Form 8283 and a Contemporaneous Letter of Acceptance.

IRS Publication 4303 is a very informative booklet available online at www.irs.gov The web site also has links to all of the forms required by the IRS to properly document any donations for a tax deduction.

We will help with any questions you or your tax professional may have regarding your charitable donation to the National Save The Sea Turtle Foundation.
